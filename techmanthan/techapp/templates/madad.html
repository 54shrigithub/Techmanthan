AD MANIA

-Dare to go Mad

Have to present an acted advertisement for selling an IT or Science related Product.

Instructions :

  1. Teams would be required to formulate a catchline for the proposed product.
  2. Different forms of expression like short skits, jingles, slogans, or any other innovative form can be incorporated.
  3. Mad-ads is not an individual event; a team should comprise a minimum of 3 members and a maximum of 6 members.
  4. The advertisement may be in Kannada, English, or Hindi Languages.
  5. Caution should be taken to refrain from displaying obscenity, violence, prejudice, defamation, etc. in advertisements.
  6. Each advertisement should not exceed the time limit of 6 minutes. In case any team exceeds the time limit, 1 mark will be deducted for each minute.
  7. The decision of the Judges will be final and binding
  8. Marking Criteria:
    ● Ability to attract an audience& attention.
    ● Concept of ad
    ● Teamwork
    ● Creativity
    ● Originality
    ● Humour
